What does This system DO
The system is capable of enrolling admitted patient to the system and that allows immediate tracking of the patients health status as soon as the have their temperature and heartrate sensors turned on to them.To admit a patient click here: Admit patient. All necessary patients details will save to the hospital data polls. To view patient admited list click the link here: Patient List The tracking system of sensor nodes save both the patient temperature and heart rates to the database of patient respectively. Report To Your supervise for any system misbehaviur

Enrolling the admitted Patient To Monitoring System.
The system have the capacity to admit all the patient whom the system will be required to monitor. Healh caregiver will be responsible to add necessary detail of patient to the system. The necessary details are Patient first name , last name , admitted ward, bed number , the health caregiver or Doctor who is in charge of a given patient. These information will be saved to a Hospital medical data pool | Hospital database. These information can be retrived at any regular basis. No patient will be monitored under this sytem unless he/she is enrolled.> For enrolled patient the healhcare will have to trigger a monitoring button to enable data record of of the given patient monitored.

The temperature and Heart Rate of Patient.
All the patient under monitoring system will have the table of their temperature and heart rate record and Here is where the evaluation of general health condition of the patient will be summerized. Artificial Intelligence, shall be responsible for data evaluation and Conclusion over the trending progress of the patient in time
